## THE SIGNAL YOU ARE LOOKING FOR

Millions of Chinese strangers stopped their scroll for something they had never seen before.
That emotional stop — at scale — is the signal.

**Comment sections are the clearest signal:**
- `在哪买` (where to buy) = desire
- `求链接` (send me the link) = desire
- `好看` (looks good) = admiration only — not enough
- `真的吗` (is this real?) = AHA triggered

Desire comments > admiration comments. Always.

## PLATFORM 1 — DOUYIN
**URL:** `douyin.com` | **Install:** APK via APKPure.com (not international TikTok)

Domestic Chinese TikTok. The algorithm surfaces viral product content to non-followers based on engagement velocity. A demo video with 2M+ likes means millions of strangers stopped for it unprompted.

### Search Hashtags — Copy These Exactly

```
#收纳神器     Storage Genius
#家居好物     Home Good Products
#创意好物     Creative Good Products
#创意家居     Creative Home
#桌面收纳     Desk Storage
#车内收纳     Car Storage / Organizer
#家居收纳     Home Storage / Organization
#布艺收纳     Fabric Storage
#礼品包装     Gift Packaging
#DIY改造      DIY Renovation / Makeover
#收纳diy      Storage DIY
#手工涂装     Hand Painting / Finishing (Semi-DIY signal)
#手工制作     Handmade / Craft
#黄铜配件     Brass Hardware / Accessories
#厨房神器     Kitchen Genius (limited — check food-grade requirement)
```

### What to Capture
- Screenshot immediately when you stop scrolling
- Save the video (long press) for strong visual AHA candidates
- Note the like count — this is your virality signal
- Note comment tone — desire vs. admiration

### Session Rule
Scroll maximum 20 minutes per session. If nothing stops you in 20 minutes — stop. Zero is the correct result most days.

## PLATFORM 2 — XIAOHONGSHU (RED NOTE)
**URL:** `xiaohongshu.com` | **Install:** Google Play (some regions) or APK

Less entertainment, more discovery. Users share products they found and genuinely loved. Format is Pinterest + purchase intent. The product images and engagement signals are fully visible without login.

### Search Terms — Copy These Exactly

```
创意收纳     Creative Storage
家居改造     Home Transformation
好物分享     Product Sharing
租房神器     Rental Genius (small space solutions)
家居收纳     Home Storage / Organization
桌面改造     Desk Makeover
收纳技巧     Storage Techniques
手工涂装     Hand Painting / Finishing
礼品创意     Gift Ideas
```

### What to Look For
- Posts with 1,000+ saves (bookmark icon) — saves indicate "I want to do this" not just "I liked this"
- "Where to buy" comments = demand signal
- Product close-up photos with transformation before/after = AHA content

## PLATFORM 3 — 1688
**URL:** `1688.com` | **Access:** Mobile browser, no app needed

The wholesale sourcing layer beneath the viral layer. Use to confirm manufacturing demand and read the pipeline clock.

### The Supplier Count Clock

| Supplier Count | What It Means | Your Action |
|---|---|---|
| 1–2 suppliers | Too early — product not yet manufacturing-ready | Hold. Check again in 7 days. |
| 3–8 suppliers | **Sweet spot — window open** | **PROCEED with urgency** |
| 10–19 suppliers | Late but possible | Verify AliExpress English listings immediately |
| 20+ at commodity pricing | Import wave starting | ABORT |

### Browse Categories — Copy These

```
家居用品 → 收纳整理     Home Goods → Storage & Organization
礼品 → 节庆用品         Gifts → Festival Supplies
文具 → 桌面收纳         Stationery → Desk Storage
工艺礼品                Craft Gifts (Semi-DIY hardware sourcing)
儿童用品 → 收纳          Children's Products → Storage
车载用品                 Car Accessories
```

### What to Look For
- Same product appearing from multiple suppliers = real manufacturing demand
- "爆款" (explosive product) label in listings
- Monthly order volumes above 500 units = validated demand at wholesale level

## PLATFORM 4 — TAOBAO
**URL:** `taobao.com` | **Access:** Mobile browser or app

Consumer retail layer. Use Google Translate camera to read listings.

**Signal:** Products with 1,000+ reviews AND not yet on AliExpress in English = sweet spot timing.

**What to avoid:** Products with "Ships internationally" in English already visible — window likely closing.

## PLATFORM 5 — WECHAT CHANNELS
**Access:** WeChat app (email registration works, no Chinese phone required for basic access)

Surfaces product content to older, higher-income demographic than Douyin. Less competition from other scanners globally in this channel. Search product categories using the same Chinese terms above.

## PLATFORM 6 — PINDUODUO
**URL:** `pinduoduo.com` | **Use:** Volume confirmation ONLY — not idea generation

Do not scan for ideas here. Quality tier is too low for AHA products.

**Only use:** If you spotted something on Douyin, check if it has 50,000+ monthly orders on Pinduoduo. If yes — Chinese mass market validated it. That confirms it escaped niche. Does not change your window timing analysis.
