# NBSV Source Intelligence — Validated Concept Mines
## Source Channel 2 — Crowdfunding & Pre-Validated Physical Products
### Open this file before every crowdfunding scan session

---

## THE STRUCTURAL ADVANTAGE OF THIS CHANNEL

**Strangers paid real money before the product existed.**

This is a fundamentally stronger signal than viral video engagement. A Kickstarter backer made a financial commitment to a product they had never held. That is a higher-intent AHA signal than any like count or comment.

What you gain from this channel that Chinese platforms cannot give you:
- External demand validation before you've seen a single video
- No timing pressure from Chinese suppliers — the product may not be on 1688 at all
- Often producible by Egyptian workshops because Kickstarter products are designed for small-batch production before mass manufacturing
- Potentially zero global reference for Egyptian market — the product may have never reached Egypt or any Middle Eastern market

## PLATFORM 1 — KICKSTARTER
**URL:** `kickstarter.com`

### Navigation Path
Discover → Design & Technology → Product Design → Sort by: Most Funded / Newly Launched

Alternatively browse: Art → Design / Technology → Hardware

### The Three Time Windows

| Window | Campaign Status | Signal Strength | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Window A** | Live campaign, 500+ backers, simple physical product | Strongest — demand proven, no supply anywhere on earth | Run full filter immediately |
| **Window B** | Successfully funded, shipping to backers (6–18 months ago) | Strong — product manufactured but likely not in Egypt | Verify Egyptian market — if clean, proceed |
| **Window C** | Funded 1–3 years ago, moderate backer count | Moderate — may have reached Amazon | Verify Egyptian market first, verify no mass distribution |

### Minimum Signal Threshold
- 500+ backers on a simple physical product (not software, not electronic-dependent)
- Successfully funded (not just launched)
- Innovation is in: geometry / material combination / clever assembly — NOT in patented mechanism

### Kill Signals Specific to Kickstarter
- Raised over $1M AND featured in Wired / TechCrunch / Verge = likely heading to mass market → verify urgently
- Core innovation is a patented mechanism you cannot replicate → KILL
- Product requires original inventor's specific manufacturing capability → KILL
- Electronics are central to the product's function → KILL (standard electronics kill applies)
- Already on Amazon with 1,000+ reviews → window closed → KILL

### What to Bookmark
Products that pass your first scan but are in Window C — bookmark and revisit with Egyptian market verification before fully rejecting. Many viable products sit in this window.

## PLATFORM 2 — INDIEGOGO
**URL:** `indiegogo.com`

More permissive than Kickstarter — flexible funding means products can launch without hitting their goal. Apply a higher bar here.

### Minimum Signal Threshold for Indiegogo
- 500+ backers minimum (higher bar than Kickstarter because flexible funding is less validated)
- Successfully funded (not just reaching flexible goal)
- Physical product with no core electronic component
- Campaign updates showing real production progress (not vaporware)

### InDemand Section — Underused Signal
Products still selling after their campaign ended appear in "InDemand." These have multi-year validated demand from strangers — some of the most reliable signals in this channel. Browse InDemand in the Design / Home categories regularly.

## PLATFORM 3 — MAKUAKE (Japan)
**URL:** `makuake.com` | **Language:** Japanese — Google Translate browser extension required

Japan's dominant product crowdfunding platform. Products here are often original Japanese designs with zero English-language coverage globally. A product with 1,000+ backers on Makuake and no AliExpress equivalent is in an earlier window than almost anything on Western platforms.

### Browse Categories
```
生活・インテリア    Lifestyle / Interior
ファッション・雑貨   Fashion / Miscellaneous goods
フード・ドリンク     Food / Drink (check food-grade requirement)
アウトドア・スポーツ Outdoor / Sports
```

### Signal Threshold
- 1,000+ backers
- Product is not yet on Alibaba, AliExpress, or Amazon

## PLATFORM 4 — GREEN FUNDING (Japan)
**URL:** `greenfunding.jp` | **Language:** Japanese — Google Translate required

Second Japanese crowdfunding platform. Narrower catalog than Makuake but occasionally surfaces genuinely novel concepts, particularly in gift packaging and small organizational products.

Browse: `生活・インテリア` and `テクノロジー` (avoid electronics-dependent items in Tech)

---

## PLATFORM 5 — CAMPFIRE (Japan)
**URL:** `camp-fire.jp` | **Language:** Japanese — Google Translate required

Third Japanese platform. Broader, includes artisan and niche products. Useful specifically for gift packaging concepts, handcraft-adjacent organizational items, and products with a premium-feel finish.

---

## PLATFORM 6 — WEFUNDER
**URL:** `wefunder.com`

US equity crowdfunding. Physical product companies raising early capital here have validated their concept enough to attract investors. Weaker signal than backer counts, but the product pipeline is 18–24 months ahead of market.

Browse: Consumer Products / Hardware / Home

Signal: Companies raising at early stage with a working prototype in a simple physical product category.

---

## PLATFORM 7 — REPUBLIC
**URL:** `republic.co`

Same logic as WeFunder. Browse Consumer / Hardware. Earlier signal than Kickstarter — less validated but further ahead in timeline.

## HOW TO READ A CROWDFUNDING LISTING FOR NBSV

When you find a candidate, extract these from the listing:

```
1. What is it? (can you describe it in one sentence?)
2. Why did backers fund it? (what problem does it solve?)
3. What is it made from? (materials in the product photos)
4. Has anyone manufactured it outside the campaign? 
   (search on AliExpress, Amazon, Google Shopping)
5. Is this visible anywhere in Egypt? 
   (standard 5-platform Egyptian verification)
6. Is the AHA visual? (would someone stop and pick this up 
   from a counter without reading the description?)
7. Could an Egyptian workshop make the core structure?
```

If any answer points to a kill — stop there. Do not advance.

## SCANNING SESSION GUIDE

**Time required:** 20–30 minutes per session

**Session flow:**
1. Kickstarter → Product Design → Newly Launched (10 min)
2. Kickstarter → Product Design → Most Funded (5 min)
3. Indiegogo → InDemand → Design (5 min)
4. Makuake → 生活・インテリア (5–10 min, Google Translate active)

**What to screenshot:** Everything where you stop and ask "why doesn't this exist in Egypt?"

**What to skip immediately:**
- Any product with electronics as a core component
- Any product that requires describing to understand
- Any product already visible on Amazon with significant reviews
