## SHOP TIERS

**Tier 1 — Proven:** Sold 3+ units in 3 days on first delivery. Priority for every new product.
**Tier 2 — Active:** Has bought at least one batch. Reliable but not proven fast-movers.
**Tier 3 — Prospect:** Has not bought yet. Target for pre-sell on next product.
**Inactive:** Did not respond to pre-sell or refused. Low priority unless new owner/context.

---

## SHOP ENTRY RULES (Do Not Deviate)

1. Place product on counter. Say nothing. Watch.
2. If the shop owner picks it up unprompted — product passed.
3. If no reaction in 2 minutes — wrong product or wrong shop. Leave without explaining.
4. Never explain the product to a shop owner or to a customer.
5. Leave the shop owner 40–60% retail margin — instant yes is the goal, not negotiation.

## ZONE EXCLUSIVITY TRACKING

When any shop commits to a product:

Record the exclusivity zone here. Do not offer conflicting zones to two shops.
