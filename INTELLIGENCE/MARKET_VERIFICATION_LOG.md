---

## WHY THIS LOG EXISTS

When you produce a product, you need to know with certainty that the Egyptian market was clean at the time you committed. If a copycat later claims they were first — this log is your timestamp.

More practically: this log shows you which kill signals appear most often in your market checks, helping you pre-screen faster over time.

---

## HOW TO LOG AN ENTRY

Run all five checks on every candidate that passes the AHA filter.
Log the result here immediately — before making any production decision.
Add new entries at the TOP (most recent first).

## WHAT "FOUND" LOOKS LIKE (Examples to recognize)

**Clear found signals — abort immediately:**
- Exact product on Jumia/Noon with Egyptian delivery
- Facebook Marketplace seller in any Egyptian city
- Instagram Egypt account selling or promoting the product
- Google Lens returning Egyptian website results
- Any physical shop you personally know carries this

**Gray area — investigate before deciding:**
- Similar but not identical product on Jumia → compare carefully. If the AHA is different and the product is genuinely different → may still advance. If it's essentially the same thing → abort.
- Product visible on international Instagram accounts but no Egyptian sellers → Egyptian market may still be clean → verify further before deciding
- Wholesale market rumors but nothing confirmed → go physically check before advancing

**When in doubt — abort.** The cost of aborting a valid candidate is zero. The cost of producing into an already-present market is a full batch of unsellable inventory.
