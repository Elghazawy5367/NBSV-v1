# NBSV Pre-Sell Script
## Exact Words for Shop Owner Conversations
### Step 5 of the 7-Step Operational Cycle

---

## THE CORE PRINCIPLE

**The video does the selling. You do the logistics.**

Do not describe the product.
Do not explain the product.
Do not show a photo.
Show the video. Say the script. Stop talking.

---

## THE SCRIPT — VIDEO PRE-SELL
### Use when you have a Chinese/Korean viral video

Walk in. Pull out your tablet. Show the video playing.
Let them watch. Say nothing while it plays.

When the video ends, say:

> **"This has [X] million views in China. Nobody in Egypt has seen it yet. I can make it here locally. Do you want to be the first shop in [area name] to carry it?"**

Then stop talking. Wait.

---

**If they say YES immediately:**

> **"I need a 50% deposit to start production — [amount in EGP]. I'll deliver [number] units in [timeline]. You keep [X]% profit on every unit you sell. And I won't supply this to any other shop within 2km of you this season."**

Collect the deposit via Vodafone Cash / Instapay / cash.
Leave. Start production.

---

**If they say "let me think about it" or hesitate:**

> **"I'm confirming orders this week only — production starts when I have 2 shops committed. After that I can't guarantee availability before [season]."**

Then stop talking. Wait again.

---

**If they say NO:**

Say nothing. Smile. Leave.
Note in `INTELLIGENCE/SHOP_NETWORK.md`: date, product, response.
Move to the next shop on your list.

---

## THE SCRIPT — PROTOTYPE PRE-SELL
### Use when you have a physical prototype (AI-originated product)

Place the prototype on the counter. Say nothing. Watch.

If they pick it up and examine it:

> **"I made this. Nobody in Egypt sells it yet. I can produce 30 units in [timeline]. Do you want to be first?"**

Same deposit script as above.

If they don't pick it up:
Note the non-reaction. The product may not have passed the AHA test at shelf level. Do not explain it. Leave.

---

## THE ZONE EXCLUSIVITY OFFER
### Add this when confirming any committing shop

> **"I won't supply this product to any other shop within 2km of you for the rest of this season."**

This converts a shop owner from a passive stockist into an active copycat sensor and territory defender — at zero cost to you.

---

## THE MYSTERY BOX OFFER
### Use for shops that want to see sell-through before committing

> **"Here are 3 units. Keep 100% of the profit on all 3. If all 3 sell in 3 days, I'll bring 30 more at wholesale price."**

Any shop that sells 3 units in 3 days is a Tier 1 distribution partner.

---

## WHAT YOU NEVER DO DURING PRE-SELL

- Never describe how the product works
- Never explain who it's for
- Never justify the price
- Never compare it to anything else
- Never say "people will love this"
- Never show photos instead of video
- Never follow up more than once after a hesitation — two follow-ups signals desperation

---

## TRACKING PRE-SELL OUTCOMES

Record every pre-sell attempt in `INTELLIGENCE/SHOP_NETWORK.md`:

```

```

---

## THE PRODUCTION TRIGGER

```
2+ shops committed + deposits collected → PRODUCE
Fewer than 2 shops committed → ABORT at zero cost
```

The deposit funds the workshop deposit.
Personal capital never at risk.
