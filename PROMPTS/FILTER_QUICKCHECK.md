# NBSV Filter — Quick Check
## 3 Questions. Stop at the First NO.
### Applies to every candidate from every source

---

## THE 3-QUESTION FILTER

Apply in this order. Stop the moment you hit NO.

---

### QUESTION 1
**Has anyone in Egypt seen this before?**

Check all five before answering YES:
```
□ Jumia Egypt — search the exact product
□ Noon Egypt — search the exact product
□ Facebook Marketplace Egypt — any seller
□ Instagram Egypt — hashtags related to the product
□ Google Lens — reverse-image search your screenshot
```

Any result found on any platform → **STOP. Reject.**
All five clean → continue to Question 2.

---

### QUESTION 2
**Does the AHA happen in 3 seconds without a word being said?**

Imagine placing the product on a shop counter among 20 other products.
A stranger walks past. Do they stop? Do they pick it up without prompting?

No sign. No description. No demo. No explanation.

The object alone.

- YES — they stop and pick it up → continue to Question 3
- UNSURE — treat as NO. Stop. Reject.
- Only after a description → **STOP. Reject.**

---

### QUESTION 3
**Can I make this in Egypt, from Egyptian materials, at a price that leaves real margin?**

Three parts — all must be YES:

**Can make it:**
```
□ Describable to a workshop owner in one sentence
□ Made from: MDF / acrylic / aluminum / fabric / foam / 
  standard hardware ONLY
□ Zero electronics, zero injection molding, zero imports
□ Maximum 6 assembly steps
□ Workshop produces it in 1–2 days
```

**Egyptian materials:**
```
□ Every single component available locally in Egypt
□ Zero imported core components
□ Finish materials available: sandpaper, stain, felt, 
  paint, knobs, ribbon
```

**Real margin:**
```
□ COGS under 80 EGP at batch 30 (standard)
   OR under 120 EGP if Semi-DIY finish adds ≥30% 
   perceived value in ≤20 min/unit
□ Can leave shop owner 40–60% retail margin
   at a price the Egyptian buyer will pay without hesitation
```

Any single NO anywhere above → **STOP. Reject.**
All YES → **ADVANCE.**

## WHAT HAPPENS AFTER ALL 3 PASS

```
→ Post in 2–3 Egyptian Facebook groups: 
  "Has anyone seen something like this? [screenshot]"
  
→ If response is "No! Where? I need this!" — STRONG signal
→ If response is silence — product AHA not triggering — ABORT
→ If response is "I've seen this at [X]" — ABORT

→ Send the viral video / prototype video to 3+ shop owners
  via WhatsApp. Use the PRESELL_SCRIPT.

→ 2+ shop owners confirm AND pay 50% deposit → PRODUCE
→ Fewer than 2 confirm → ABORT at zero cost
```

**The deposit is the only real demand signal. Everything before it is signal. The deposit is confirmation.**

## WHAT "AHA" MEANS AND WHAT IT DOESN'T

| This IS AHA | This is NOT AHA |
|---|---|
| "I need this right now" | "That's clever" |
| "I know exactly who I'm buying this for" | "Interesting design" |
| "I have to show this to someone" | "I can see how that would be useful" |
| Picking it up without being asked | Looking at it and moving on |
| Asking the price immediately | Nodding and walking away |

If your honest reaction is in the right column — it failed. Move on.

---

## COMMON KILL PATTERNS (Learn These)

The fastest rejections come from recognizing these immediately:

- **"Once people understand it..."** → it requires explanation → instant kill
- **"It would work great for..."** (then listing specific conditions) → too contextual → instant kill  
- **Electronics anywhere** → instant kill, no exceptions
- **"Similar things exist but not exactly this"** → close enough is present → verify carefully
- **Core material must be sourced from China/abroad** → instant kill
- **More than 6 steps to assemble** → instant kill
- **Only useful once** → not a physical retail product → instant kill
- **"Interesting" is your honest first reaction** → not AHA → instant kill
