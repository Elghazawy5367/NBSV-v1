# NBSV Session Log
## Running Record — Every Session, Every Candidate, Every Decision
### One line per candidate. Most recent at top.

---

## HOW TO LOG

Add one line per candidate immediately after each session.
Do not batch-log after multiple sessions — precision degrades.
This log is the audit trail for the entire system.

---

## LOG FORMAT

```
[DATE] | [SOURCE] | [PRODUCT — 5 words max] | [VERDICT] | [KILL SIGNAL or NEXT STEP]
```

**Verdict options:** PASS / FAIL / INVESTIGATE / ABORT (pre-sell failed) / COMPLETED

**Example entries:**
```
12/03/2026 | Douyin | Magnetic wall cable holder | FAIL | ELECTRONICS
12/03/2026 | Kickstarter | Modular MDF desk organizer | INVESTIGATE | Market verification pending
11/03/2026 | AI-Claude | Ramadan tray divider system | PASS | Pre-sell initiated — 3 shops contacted
10/03/2026 | Yanko Design | Acrylic gift box with ribbon | FAIL | ALREADY ON JUMIA
09/03/2026 | r/somebodymakethis | Hanging entrance bag organizer | PASS | Pre-sell confirmed 2 shops — producing
```
