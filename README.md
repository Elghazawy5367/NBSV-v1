## The 3-Question Filter

Applied to every candidate from every source:

1. **Has anyone in Egypt seen this before?** — Verify: Jumia, Noon, Facebook, Instagram, physical shops
2. **Does the AHA happen in 3 seconds without a word being said?** — Stranger picks it up unprompted from a counter
3. **Can I make this in Egypt, from Egyptian materials, at a price that leaves real margin?** — One sentence to a workshop owner. All local. COGS under 80 EGP standard / 120 EGP Semi-DIY

All three YES → pre-sell to 3 shop owners. 2 deposits → produce. Fewer → abort at zero cost.

## The 7-Step Operational Cycle

```
Step 1: SCAN         → Any of the 5 source channels
Step 2: FILTER       → 3-question filter above
Step 3: VERIFY       → Jumia / Noon / Facebook / Instagram / physical shops
Step 4: SOCIAL       → Post in 2–3 Egyptian Facebook groups: "Has anyone seen this?"
Step 5: PRE-SELL     → Send video to 3+ shop owners. 2+ say YES + deposit → produce.
Step 6: PRODUCE      → Workshop job. Inspect first 3 units via WhatsApp before full batch.
Step 7: DISTRIBUTE   → Deliver. Place on counter. Say nothing. Watch.
```

## The 12 Rules That Cannot Be Broken

1. Never produce until 2+ shop owners have confirmed via WhatsApp deposit after seeing the video
2. Never explain the product — to anyone. If it needs explaining, it failed
3. Never use personal capital for unvalidated production — shop deposits fund workshop deposits
4. Never produce a product found anywhere in the Egyptian market in any form
5. Never miss the QC checkpoint — inspect first 3 units via WhatsApp before approving full batch
6. Never carry inventory into the next season — abandon cleanly, protect working capital
7. Never reveal to workshops what you're selling or where — protect market intelligence
8. Never treat "interesting" as equivalent to "AHA" — completely different thresholds
9. Never let a product you love override the filter — the filter is objective, love is not
10. Never validate demand using Facebook polls — only shop deposits validate
11. For Semi-DIY: Never start finishing until the base structure is inspected and approved
12. Never let any layer of rules override the core: NBSV + AHA + Producible + Real WTP
